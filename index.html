Meet the Candidate

Candidate Name’s lifelong commitment to public service is rooted in years of community work, neighborhood advocacy, and partnership with families, youth, and local organizations.

Raised in a working-class family and shaped by strong community values, Candidate Name learned early that safe parks, walkable streets, and welcoming public spaces are essential to a healthy neighborhood. Over the years, they have helped bring people together to support youth programs, improve park access, and create safer routes for walking and biking.

Today, Candidate Name is running for Park Board to ensure every family has access to vibrant, well-maintained parks and thoughtful leadership that listens, collaborates, and gets things done.

Experience

Youth Coach & Mentor

Years of experience working directly with young people on bike safety, outdoor education, and leadership—helping kids build confidence and connect to their parks.

Bikeway & Trails Advocate

A strong voice for safe streets, better trail connections, and equitable access to parks for people walking, rolling, and biking across the city.

Community Leader

Deep experience on neighborhood boards and advisory groups, working on infrastructure, housing, transportation, and park investments that serve families.

Priorities

Infrastructure & Environment

  • Modernize park buildings, playgrounds, and athletic fields.
  • Protect the river, trees, and urban wildlife habitat.
  • Complete key trail connections and fill sidewalk gaps.

Youth, Families & Communities

  • Expand quality, affordable youth programs and camps.
  • Strengthen partnerships with schools and community groups.
  • Support youth employment and leadership opportunities.

Respect & Collaboration

  • Stabilize staffing at rec centers and regional parks.
  • Empower local park staff to tailor programs to neighbors.
  • Work closely with city, county, and community partners.
